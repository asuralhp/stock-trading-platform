# Trading Fees & Commissions

At Leo's Stock, we believe in transparent pricing. Our fee structure is designed to be competitive for both high-frequency traders and long-term investors.

## Stock & ETF Trading

| Trade Type | Standard Account | Pro Account | Institutional |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **U.S. Listed Stocks** | $0.00 / trade | $0.00 / trade | $0.00 / trade |
| **ETFs** | $0.00 / trade | $0.00 / trade | $0.00 / trade |
| **OTC Bulletin Board** | $6.95 / trade | $4.95 / trade | Negotiated |

## Options Trading

- **Equity & Index Options**: $0.65 per contract (Standard), $0.50 per contract (Pro).
- **Exercise & Assignment**: $0.00.

## Margin Rates

Margin rates are based on the base rate (currently 5.25%) plus a spread.

| Debit Balance | Standard Spread | Pro Spread |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| $0 - $25,000 | +1.50% | +1.00% |
| $25,000 - $100,000 | +1.25% | +0.75% |
| $100,000+ | +1.00% | +0.50% |

## Other Fees

- **Wire Transfer (Outgoing)**: $25.00
- **Returned Check**: $30.00
- **Paper Statements**: $5.00 / month (Free for electronic)
- **Inactivity Fee**: $0.00 (We do not charge inactivity fees)

*Fees are subject to change without notice. Please refer to the latest fee schedule.*
