# About Leo's Stock

Leo's Stock was built to democratize access to institutional-grade trading tools. By combining traditional technical analysis with modern natural language processing (NLP) and deep learning, we provide a robust framework for algorithmic trading.

## Our Mission

To provide a transparent, extensible, and powerful trading platform for developers and data scientists.

## The Team

We are a group of passionate developers, data scientists, and traders working together to build the future of automated trading.

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js, React
- **Backend**: Python, FastAPI
- **Database**: MongoDB, Vector DB
- **AI/ML**: PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Transformers
- **Infrastructure**: Docker, Kubernetes
