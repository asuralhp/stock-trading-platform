# Account Types

Choose the account tier that best fits your trading style and volume.

## Standard Account

Perfect for beginners and casual investors.

- **Minimum Deposit**: $0
- **Market Data**: Real-time (15-min delayed for OTC)
- **Leverage**: Up to 2:1 (Reg T Margin)
- **API Access**: Read-only (Portfolio tracking)
- **Support**: Email support (24h response time)

## Pro Account

Designed for active traders and algorithmic trading enthusiasts.

- **Minimum Deposit**: $25,000
- **Market Data**: Real-time Level 1 & Level 2
- **Leverage**: Up to 4:1 (Day Trading Margin)
- **API Access**: Full Trading API (Rate limited to 100 req/min)
- **Support**: Priority Email & Chat
- **Features**: Access to advanced AI prediction models.

## Institutional Account

For hedge funds, family offices, and high-volume proprietary trading firms.

- **Minimum Deposit**: $1,000,000
- **Market Data**: Direct Market Access (DMA)
- **Leverage**: Portfolio Margin (Risk-based)
- **API Access**: High-frequency API (Colocation available)
- **Support**: Dedicated Account Manager & 24/7 Phone Support
- **Features**: Custom model integration and dedicated server instances.
